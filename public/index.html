Class D/E 3.sem Spring Group TechNice Members Jonathan, Cathrine, Mari, Jens and Nikolaj
Disclaimer Due to constraints when working on the project and due to being allowed another week during spring break we have decided to add more phases to our sprint breakdown
Phase one (Monday -Tuesday): Who was active during this sprint? All group members were active to some extent during this sprint
Who did what, during this sprint? We set up github, made mockups and started doing basic travis setup for the development environment.
Jens made mockups. Jonathan was in charge of Travis and backend setup. Everyone else was on zoom helping when questions arose
We began implementing entities and DTO’s in the backend project and talked about the structure of the application.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? We got the development environment set up (Travis, databases, netbeans project and a repo on github )
Phase two (Wednesday-Thursday): Who was active during this sprint? Mari, Cathrine, Jens and Jonathan
Who did what, during this sprint? On wednesday Jens, Mari and Cathrine worked separately on the frontend. Jonathan finished the setup of all the entity classes and their relations.
We started to implement relations between the entities for the database in the backend project and set up the hobby and zip codes scripts. In the backend project Cathrine started to implement the update person function. At this time it only updated the person’s email, first name and last name. Cathrine began working on implementing Error Handling.
Mari made some small fixes to the hobby-class, after having added the script of hobbies, whereafter our predefined hobbyclass no longer fit.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? We made some basic templates for how the website would look as well as setting up some connections that would work when the backend was finished
Phase three (Friday-Sunday): Who was active during this sprint? Jens, Jonathan and Cathrine
Who did what, during this sprint? Jens made some endpoints and facade methods that were missing and implemented them, with assistance from Cathrine. Jonathan also made facade methods, as well as a basic layout for most of the frontend.
Cathrine and Nicolai implemented deletePerson() on the backend and made some tests.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? Some of the frontend methods were beginning to work during this sprint. Although a lot of them were missing and we had several issues with cors, where it would not allow us to fetch from our api. Phase four (Monday -Tuesday): Who was active during this sprint? Jens, Jonathan og Cathrine
Who did what, during this sprint? Jens added table functionality on the page, adding fetch and plotting data into the persons table with help from Jonathan. Jonathan also implemented a few more of the table functionalities - e.g. “search for person by id”, “search for person by phone” and “get amount of people with a given hobby”.
Cathrine was advising during discord-meetings. Neither her backend nor her frontend were able to run at this point in time.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? A working site on the frontend, more or less, as well as editing the navbar to match our entities Phase five (Wednesday -Thursday): Who was active during this sprint? Cathrine, Jens and Jonathan
Who did what, during this sprint? Cathrine and Jens worked together on implementing find person by hobby in the frontend. Jonathan fixed some problems caused by the server when fetching in the frontend, and furthermore began working on “delete person by id”, “create person” and “edit person”.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? Implemented “search person by hobby functionality”, “delete person by id”
Phase six (Friday -Sunday): Who was active during this sprint? Jonathan, Jens and Cathrine
Who did what, during this sprint? Jonathan implemented displaying a list of hobbies, as well as a list of addresses, when typing in either kind of field. Jens assisted Jonathan and Cathrine in coding on the frontend
In the backend project Cathrine further developed “update person” to update phones, addresses, ect. on a person in the database. Cathrine started up some tests for “update person”.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? Almost all of the remaining endpoints were implemented in some form on the frontend, some tests were made
Phase seven (Monday -Tuesday): Who was active during this sprint? Jonathan og Cathrine
Who did what, during this sprint? Jonathan made frontend functions for creating and editing users work with multiple asynchronous fetches with help from Cathrine. Cathrine worked on the backend implementation of these, with a bit of help the other way around. Cathrine also worked a lot with making tests work after the introduction of the frontend system.
What did your team accomplish during this spring? A more or less finished product with minor bugs. It does however lack a bit of error handling.
